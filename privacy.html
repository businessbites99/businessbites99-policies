Privacy Policy

Effective 14 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how BusinessBites99 (“we”, “us”) handles personal information when you use the BusinessBites99 desktop application and this website. Contact us at businessbites99@gmail.com.

1. Information we handle

Depending on how you use the Service, the application may handle:

  • TikTok authorisation information, including access and refresh tokens and an account identifier supplied by TikTok;
  • video files, captions, hashtags and related metadata that you select for upload;
  • local records of upload status, timestamps and TikTok publishing identifiers, used to prevent duplicate uploads; and
  • information you provide when contacting us for support.

2. How and why we use information

We use this information to authenticate your chosen TikTok account, perform uploads you explicitly request, show upload status, prevent accidental duplicates, maintain security and respond to support requests. We do not use the application’s data for advertising, profiling or automated decisions with legal or similarly significant effects.

3. Legal basis

Where data-protection law applies, we process information to provide the Service you request and for our legitimate interests in operating, securing and improving the Service. Where consent is required, you may withdraw it by disconnecting TikTok access or contacting us.

4. Local storage and retention

The desktop application is designed to keep authorisation tokens and upload-history records on the user’s own computer. Authorisation data remains until it expires, is replaced, or the user logs out or deletes it. Upload-history records and local videos remain until the user deletes them. Support correspondence is retained only as long as reasonably necessary to resolve the enquiry and meet legal obligations.

5. Sharing and international processing

When you request an upload, the selected video and related technical information are sent to TikTok. TikTok processes that information under its own privacy policy. Our website host may process ordinary technical logs, such as IP address and browser information, to deliver and secure these webpages. These providers may process data in other countries using the safeguards described in their own privacy documentation. We do not sell personal information.

6. Your choices and rights

You can choose which videos to upload, decline an upload, disconnect TikTok access, and delete locally stored tokens and upload records. Depending on your location and applicable law, you may also have rights to access, correct, erase, restrict or object to processing, and to receive a portable copy of relevant information. Contact us to exercise a right.

Your right to object: where we rely on legitimate interests, you may object to that processing by contacting us.

7. Complaints

Please contact us first so we can try to resolve your concern. If you are in the United Kingdom, you may also complain to the Information Commissioner’s Office at ico.org.uk. People elsewhere may contact their local data-protection authority.

8. Security and children

We use reasonable safeguards and keep sensitive authorisation data out of the public policy website. No system is completely secure. The Service is not directed to children below the minimum age required to use TikTok in their location.

9. Updates

We may update this policy when the Service, providers or legal requirements change. Material changes will be reflected on this page and the effective date will be updated.

10. Contact

Email privacy questions or requests to businessbites99@gmail.com.
